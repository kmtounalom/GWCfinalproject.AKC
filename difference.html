How to make a difference



Teens:

Being apart of underrepresented minorities can be hard sometimes and it can really change your mood and how you view your life so here are some things you can do when you feel like you're alone in this big world.

  • Staying true to yourself is the most important thing when it comes to this stuff. You may feel lost and unsure of your path when you try to look for people to look up to but none of them look like you, so you can't relate. But this website will show you that there are amazing successful people out there, and they do look like you.

  • Another thing you should know is that it's okay to feel alone. This is normal. Try connecting with other girls within minorities to share your experiences together and reflect. This is a great way to gain support. You may not know this, but there are people experiecning the same thing you are.


Here's a list of programs you can do to truly find your passion, whether its law, medicine, or computer science, or even just connecting with people by identity.

  • Girls Who Code
  • Portfolio Project
  • SYEP Internships (Youth Employment)
  • Young Executives Of Color (YEOC)
  • INSIGHT High School Program
  • Making Connections
