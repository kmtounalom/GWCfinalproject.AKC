Teens

Being apart of underrepresented minorities can be hard sometimes and it can really change your mood and how you view your life so here are some things you can do when you feel like you're alone in this big world.

  • Be a role model! This is the most important thing you can do to help. Having someone to look up to, doing all the right things, can be influential on your child, especially when a relationship is already established.
  • As well as being a role model, make a safe space to talk about feelings. Sometimes your child may not know you can talk about things, such as feelings. This topic is uncomfortable for young children so once you welcome it with open arms it'll be easier to talk about it on your child's part

    • Parents/Guardians:

    Hello parents and guardians! Or really any adult figure in a child's life. You may feel or see your child struggling but not know how to help or if you even should. Here's a list we strongly encoueage you follow.

    • Be a role model! This is the most important thing you can do to help. Having someone to look up to, doing all the right things, can be influential on your child, especially when a relationship is already established.

    • As well as being a role model, make a safe space to talk about feelings. Sometimes your child may not know you can talk about things, such as feelings. This topic is uncomfortable for young children so once you welcome it with open arms it'll be easier to talk about it on your child's part

    • Another thing is to share your experiences. Your child may alone in some situations, and if you guys have an experience that overlaps eachother then you can share your knowledge specifcally on that. Even if the situation isn't similar, sharing things that you went through will show your child that you went through hard times as well, and that you perservered, and still made it in life.
