WEvision




Our goal is to educate and shed light on the under-representation of women minorities. With the skills we learned from the GWC SIP program, we learned how to use our resources and oppurtunities to learn about current topics that has to do equal representation within our community. We believe that gender and ethnicity is an important issue that needs to be touched on, and worked on so that we can bring awareness to a topic that should be important to all of us.
