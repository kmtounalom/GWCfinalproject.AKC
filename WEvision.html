WEvision


Growing up, one of the most empowering things as a young girl is envisioning your future self through someone who looks like you and has already done what you want to do. A major issue with this is that often enough the media doesn’t represent everyone equally. This puts girls in underrepresented minorities at an unfair disadvantage. At the surface, this issue may seem minor but subconsciously it makes the difference. Our goal with this website is to empower girls of all ethnicities to believe that whatever future they envision for themselves, whether it’s clear now or not, is obtainable. Our website gives girls easy access to read about role models, both old and young, in various career paths. A career quiz is also included to help girls see their current potential. Also there are various book recommendations and links for girls to start taking action for seizing their future now.
